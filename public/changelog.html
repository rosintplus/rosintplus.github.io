Privacy & Removal Requests

Effective: May 26, 2026

rosint.dev has no backend, no accounts, and does not store your searches.

On this site we use:

  • Google Analytics 4: cookies and visit metrics. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Vercel Analytics: cookieless visit counts, plus standard server logs from Vercel hosting. See Vercel's privacy policy.

When you search: your browser sends the query directly to Arctic Shift and PullPush. These requests do not pass through our server. Arctic Shift does not publish a privacy policy. By searching, you accept that.

Your rights (PIPEDA, GDPR, Quebec Law 25, and similar): access, correction, deletion, or complaint to a regulator. Contact: zuxu4n@proton.me.

Removals: email rosint-removal@proton.me with your username. I can restrict an account from being searched, but I cannot remove individual posts or comments, and I am not legally obligated to remove anything. For data removal at the source, contact the services directly: PullPush and ArcticShift.
